  • Primary: Main color for logos, buttons, and key brand elements.
  • Accent: For calls to action, highlights, or decorative areas.
  • Muted: Backgrounds or UI panels to reduce visual noise.
  • Neutral: Used in base UI, text, dividers, or cards.
  • Secondary: To complement or alternate with your primary color.
